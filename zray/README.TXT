## About this Plugin

This plugin provides access to the following information about your Concrete5 install:

* The current page's blocks, including whether they were served from the cache, (5.7.5 and greater), the area they came from, whether they were rendered, and their corresponding searchable content, and how long they took to render.
* Which page objects were requested from the current page.
* Which config values were requested from the current page, their values, and how many times they were requested.
* Which custom permissions are in place on a particular page
* The current user's access entities, including which groups they belong to, whether they  quality as a "file uploader" and more.
